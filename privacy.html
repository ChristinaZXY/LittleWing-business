1.Who we are

LittleWing ("LittleWing," "we," "us," or "our") is an iOS application operated by [Plasticity LLC]. LittleWing helps parents through the early years with Wingie, an AI companion, and a curated shelf of recommended products.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use the LittleWing app and related services (the "Services"), how we use and share it, and the choices you have. By using LittleWing, you agree to the practices described here.

2.Information we collect

Information you provide to us

  • Account information. When you create an account, we collect your email address and, if you use Sign in with Apple, the name and email (or Apple-relayed email) you choose to share. We do not receive your Apple password.
  • Profile information. Details you choose to add, such as your child's first name, birth date or age, and developmental stage, used to personalize guidance and product recommendations.
  • Conversations with Wingie. The questions, messages, and content you send to Wingie, our AI companion feature.
  • Saved items. Products you add to your cart or wishlist.
  • Communications. Information you provide when you contact us for support.

Information collected automatically

  • Usage data. How you interact with the app — screens viewed, features used, products tapped, and affiliate links clicked.
  • Device information. Device type, operating system version, app version, and general diagnostic data.
  • Identifiers. An account or device identifier used to associate activity with your account.

3.How we use information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Services, including Wingie and product recommendations;
  • Personalize guidance and suggested products based on the stage and preferences you share;
  • Create and manage your account and authenticate you;
  • Respond to your questions and provide customer support;
  • Understand how the app is used so we can improve it;
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues, fraud, or abuse; and
  • Comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use the content of your Wingie conversations to serve you third-party advertising.

4.Wingie & AI features

Wingie is powered by a third-party AI provider, [AI PROVIDER]. When you send a message to Wingie, your message (and relevant context needed to answer it) is transmitted to that provider to generate a response, and then returned to you in the app.

We instruct our AI provider to process this content only to provide the feature. Depending on the provider, your inputs may be retained for a limited period for safety and abuse-prevention purposes, but are not used to train their models where we have selected enterprise or zero-retention terms that exclude such use.

5.How we share information

We share information only as described below. We do not sell your personal information.

  • Service providers. We use trusted vendors to run LittleWing, including [AI PROVIDER] (Wingie), our backend and authentication provider (Supabase), and analytics tooling. They may process your information only to provide services to us and under obligations of confidentiality.
  • Affiliate and merchant partners. When you tap a product link, you are taken to a third-party retailer (such as Amazon). Your activity on that retailer's site is governed by their privacy policy, not ours. See Section 6.
  • Legal and safety. We may disclose information if required by law, or to protect the rights, safety, and security of our users, the public, or LittleWing.
  • Business transfers. If LittleWing is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

6.Affiliate links & products

LittleWing participates in affiliate programs, including the Amazon Associates Program. This means we may earn a commission when you purchase products through links in the app, at no additional cost to you.

When you tap a product, we may record that interaction (for example, which product and when) to understand which recommendations are useful and to attribute qualifying purchases. The retailer you are sent to operates independently and collects and uses your information under its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review the privacy practices of any retailer before making a purchase.

7.Data retention

We keep your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Services. We retain and use information as necessary to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. When you delete your account, we delete or de-identify your personal information within a reasonable period, except where we are required to retain it.

9.Children's privacy

LittleWing is intended for use by parents and caregivers who are adults (18 or older). It is not directed to children, and we do not knowingly allow children to create accounts or use the Services directly.

LittleWing is designed for parents to use, and any information about a child is provided by the parent or caregiver (for example, a child's first name or age) to personalize guidance. We use that information only to provide the Services and do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children.

If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at [privacy@yourdomain.com] and we will take appropriate steps to delete it.
