About us

We're building the support new parents wish they had.

LittleWing started with a simple observation: the early months are full of questions, and the answers are scattered across forums, group chats, and half-remembered advice. We set out to gather that support into one calm place — a companion that's there at 3 a.m. and a shelf you don't have to second-guess.

We're a small team that cares deeply about getting the details right, especially when it comes to your family's privacy and trust.
